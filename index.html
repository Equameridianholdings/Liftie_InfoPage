About Liftie

Liftie is a professional, tech-driven cost-recovery network for corporate carpooling. Built on the philosophy of Personal Mastery — taking control of your journey through hands-on innovation — we exist to make your daily commute fair, efficient, and financially smart.

We connect verified corporate professionals for safe, door-to-door, cost-effective commuting. Drivers recover vehicle running costs. Riders pay a transparent, distance-based fare. The automated math does the rest — no haggling, no guesswork.

Liftie is about reclaiming your time and finances through a community-driven, fair-share economy. Every seat filled is a car removed. Every rand saved is a step toward financial freedom on the road.
