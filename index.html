What is Liftie - Carpool & Bikepool

We all like going together. Liftie will help you connect with other like-minded verified professionals who are traveling on the same route at the same time. Liftie provides rides for your daily commute, inter-city travel, and weekend getaways. With over 5 million users, Liftie helps save money, reduce traffic congestion, and protect the environment.

Whether you're driving or looking for a ride, Liftie makes carpooling simple, safe, and social. Join thousands of commuters who have already discovered a better way to travel.
